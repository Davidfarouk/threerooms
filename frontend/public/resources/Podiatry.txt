

A Podiatrist is a state registered professional in a branch of medicine devoted to the diagnosis, medical and surgical treatment of the disorders of the foot and ankle and lower extremity.
Diagnosis & Treatment of : 
•	Foot, Knee or Ankle Pain & Injury
•	Back Pain
•	Ligament Tears
•	Heel Pain
•	Nail Infection
•	Toe Deformity
•	Limb Pain Dysfunction
•	Corns & Callus
•	Sports Injury
•	Posture
Range of Podiatry services :
•	Prescription Insoles and Orthotics 
•	Taping and strapping of Acute Trauma
•	Ultrasound
